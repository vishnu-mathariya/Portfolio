  1. Open: /app/globals.css

  5. Save & refresh browser

  1. Open: /app/layout.tsx
  2. Find: title: 'Developer Portfolio...'
  3. Change to your preferred title

🔗 Update Navigation Links:
  1. Open: /lib/constants.ts
  2. Find: navLinks array
  3. Update href values if needed

🎯 Change Primary Color:
  1. Open: /app/globals.css
  2. Find: --primary: #a855f7;
  3. Replace with any hex color
